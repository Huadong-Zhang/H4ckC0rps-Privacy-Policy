Privacy Policy for H4ckC0rps

Privacy and Anonymity

Your privacy is our priority. H4ckC0rps does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers.

The game is designed to be played locally on your device. No account registration is required, and no user activity logs are sent to any remote servers. You can enjoy the game with the confidence that your digital footprint is not being tracked.

No Data Collection

Unlike many other applications, we do not use cookies, third-party analytics, or advertising trackers. We do not request access to your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or location services.

Because we do not collect any data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed in a data breach from our side, nor is there any data to be sold to third parties.

Data Usage and Disclosure

Since H4ckC0rps does not collect user data, there is no data to use or disclose.

  • We do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not share data with law enforcement agencies (as we possess none).
  • We do not use data for marketing purposes.

Changes and Contact

Although we do not plan to change our "no data collection" philosophy, any future updates to this policy will be posted on this page. By continuing to use the application after those changes become effective, you agree to the revised policy.

This privacy policy is effective immediately and has no expiration date.
